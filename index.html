IDENTIFI VISION STATEMENT

Why we do what we do.

What we do

We help companies retain their top
talent. We do this by enabling them to
create clear, constant and authentic
communication channels. These
channels are used to measure their
performance and provide feedback,
support and recognition. Helping to
foster deeper and meaningful
relationships.

Why we do it

The fact that more than 77% of
individuals are actively disengaged at
work baffles us. We come to work
everyday playing our small role to ensure
that the companies we work with are
able to reverse this trend. Providing
purpose—the yearning for our work to
serve something larger than ourselves.

Who we are

We are a group of entrepreneurs and software developers who started IDENTIFI to solve our own challenges with attracting, recruiting and retain top talent at our previous comapanies. We
live and breathe on IDENTIFI’s core
vision and look forward to spending the
rest of lives dedicated to it.
